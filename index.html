TECHTRIS

TECH-TRIS is an annual inter-collegiate cultural and technical festival which is being held in R.D. National College of Arts and Commerce & W.A College of Science and is being organized by the department of Information Technology.

The festival is a three-day extravaganza, with competitions in various technical and cultural events, and a host of exciting exhibits and attractions. The said festival will thus provide an opportunity to both the industry professionals & the academic professionals to identify the skills.

The festival is being hosted by the students of the prestigious RD.National college. The preparations are in full swing, and the excitement is palpable. The teams from all the participating colleges are putting in their best efforts to win the coveted prizes.


Come join us at the Tech-Tris festival, and be part of the excitement!
